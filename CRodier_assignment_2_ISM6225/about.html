About The Author

Corbin Rodier

I am a grad student at USF persuing a masters in Business Analytics and AI. I have an interest in Electric Cars, and I will share with you a little about my experience making this website. I used vsc to code in HTML for the structure of the website and used css to style the website. I utilized W3schools online information about coding in HTML and CSS, as well as resources provided by Prof. Clinton. Further, ChatGPT was a powerful tool to help tweak my code as well as find new ways of completing the website. This was an amazing experience and I am already looking forward to making my next webpage.

I hope to improve upon certain areas such as using more pictures, drawing from great free resources such as freeimages.com, etc. I hope to improve upon my colorscheme to make it more friendly to night time users.
